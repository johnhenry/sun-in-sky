## One-Week Lesson Sequence

### Day 1: Introduction (15 min)
- Demo basic features
- Show summer vs. winter comparison
- Students explore with presets

### Day 2: Latitude Investigation (30 min)
- Compare different latitudes
- Focus on equator vs. mid-lat vs. polar
- Document patterns

### Day 3: Seasonal Patterns (30 min)
- Use 365-day view
- Investigate how seasons emerge
- Connect to Earth's tilt

### Day 4: Extreme Phenomena (30 min)
- Midnight sun and polar night
- Arctic Circle investigation
- "What if?" scenarios

### Day 5: Assessment & Application (30 min)
- Student presentations
- Written assessment
- Real-world connections
